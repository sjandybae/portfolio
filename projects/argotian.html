Argotian

August 2019 | VR + AI Dialogue System

Main Role: Dialogue Writer

Argotian is a language learning system where users engage in conversation with intelligent characters capable of responding to human speech. To that end, a unique dialogue system was needed to interpret free-form speech and return a characteristically appropriate response that is relevant to each point in the story.

I joined this project as a writer of this AI-driven dialogue after the initial design for the dialogue system was already implemented. After some experience in the entire production cycle for this unique content, I also helped optimize our processes, including re-evaluating the design for cost and scalability.

Production

My contributions began with first getting an overview of the game’s established narrative, especially in regards to each character’s role and knowledge in it, and reviewing a protocol on writing dialogue for the AI system at the time. Within a day, I was writing dialogue into a spreadsheet, having to always stay ahead of my co-workers handling quality assurance, adaptation, or implementation.

Integration

Due to my experience with Unity, I was next involved in adding the dialogue content I helped write into the actual dialogue system. This unfortunately required my co-workers and me to manually enter and link up each line of data into the system, keeping track of and merging our work via Git branches.

Even after we completed the data entry, new feature requirements would emerge from the rest of the pipeline, causing incompatibility issues we would need to manually resolve. We eventually succeeded in bringing the entire system to a working, testable state without neglecting to document the challenges we overcame and protocol improvements we would do next time.

Testing

Concurrently with dialogue system development, I began the first wave of user testing. Being the one who wrote a majority of the characters’ responses, I was in a good position to look at player questions, and determine what responses the characters could answer them with. I repeatedly optimized the AI to better handle free-form speech, especially numerous ways to ask the same question.

Reflections

Having been through every part of the production pipeline for the dialogue system’s content, I eventually began to propose and plan out new feature updates that would address each of our overall system's weaknesses. From doing this, I became something like an expert among my co-workers for anything related to the dialogue system and flow logic.

Before joining Virtro, I had no experience with AI training, Unity dialogue systems, or even Google Sheets in a deep capacity. It was through working with other knowledgeable and patient professionals that I was able to learn how each system worked fast enough to make significant contributions.
