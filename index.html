At SFU's School of Interactive Arts and Technology, I studied and practiced artistic expression through various technology. I made graphics, interactive films, board games, animations, documentaries, and (for this one class) a fake "high-tech" garbage can.

Through my experiences in work and play, nothing brings me greater joy than playing a fun, narrative-driven video game except for working with other skillful creators to make such projects. Games today embody our culture, ideals, and emotional experiences, and I want to be behind the scenes making lots of them.
