Pitcher and the Whale

April 2020 | 2D Platformer

Main Role: Project Manager
Subroles: Programmer, Narrative Designer, Level Designer

  • Game Design

  • Leadership

  • Documentation

For the Ludum Dare 46 game jam, three friends and I worked remotely on a platformer game from design to submission within 72 hours. The game jam's theme being "keep it alive", "Pitcher and the Whale" has the player control Pitcher, a little fox with a bucket exploring the mountainous area and bringing water back to a dehydrating whale.

One of my teammates had some premade code and the most experience working with Gamemaker Studio 2, so my role only involved some coding when I was not working on tracking and dividing our team's tasks and production pipeline, organizing playtesting sessions, collaborating on level design, and documenting our progress for portfolio entries such as this.

"Pitcher and the Whale" was created from the bottom up within 72 hours for #ludumdare46. Check out how each feature and mechanic got added one at a time!
