Meteor Striker

December 2019 | 2.5D Action Platformer

Main Role: Lead Programmer
Subroles: Project Manager, Narrative Designer

  • Game Design

  • Leadership

  • Storytelling

  • Unity

For an advanced game design course, I worked with three peers to make a Unity-based game that would challenge the conventions of an existing game genre, which in our case was platforming. The result of 4 months of design and development was "Meteor Striker", a game where you run and jump around the map to get mysterious swords, and use them to destroy incoming meteors before they hit the ground.

Being the most experienced with Unity programming, I created almost all of the C# scripts for the game, including both platforming physics and state-machine logic. I also acted as team leader, managing our team's production pipeline, defining our core game design and narrative, and documenting these processes as part of our course's requirements.
